# ⚽ Rate the 90

> *Letterboxd for football. Log matches. Rate them. Build your history.*

---

## What is Rate the 90?

Rate the 90 is a community platform for football fans to log every match they've watched, rate it, write reviews, and track their viewing history across clubs, competitions, and seasons — just like Letterboxd does for films.

Whether it's a 0-0 slog you suffered through or a Champions League classic you'll never forget, Kickoff is where you record it.

---

## Features

- 📋 **Match Logging** — Log any match from any competition, past or present
- ⭐ **Ratings & Reviews** — Rate matches out of 5 and write short or long-form reviews
- 📅 **Viewing History** — A personal timeline of every match you've watched
- 🏆 **Competition Tracking** — Follow entire tournaments and see how many games you've caught
- 👤 **Club Profiles** — Deep-dive into any club's match history and community ratings
